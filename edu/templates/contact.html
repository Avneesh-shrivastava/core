Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to things students ask us most

How do I enroll in a course?

Go to the Courses page, click "Buy Now" on the course you want, complete the payment, and you're instantly enrolled.

Can I watch videos for free?

Yes — the first module of every course is free to watch. Purchase the course to unlock all remaining videos.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact us and we'll process a full refund.

How long do I have access after purchasing?

Lifetime access. Once you purchase a course, you can watch it as many times as you want with no expiry.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, Debit/Credit Cards, Net Banking, Paytm, PhonePe, and Google Pay via Razorpay.

Can I access courses on mobile?

Yes — Clarity Edge works on all devices including mobile phones, tablets, and desktops.
